Pinellas County Services Viewer

Welcome to the Pinellas County Services Viewer!

The Pinellas County Services Viewer (PCSV) is an unofficial (i.e., not officially affiliated with any government) interactive map of various services operated by Pinellas County (and local governments) powered by Leaflet (a JavaScript library) and OpenStreetMap (an open-source community-led mapping project).

With PCSV, you can easily find and learn about the many services Pinellas County has to offer (e.g, parks, libraries, recreation centers, etc.). See below to get started...

Using PCSV

Below is a map highlighting various locales and services operated by either the County itself or local governments within the County (Largo, Seminole, Clearwater, etc.). The map itself is provided by OpenStreetMap. For help, go to the section Guide & Troubleshooting below.

Guide & Troubleshooting

How to use PCSV on desktop

To navigate and move around the map, you can hold left click and drag the mouse to the desired location. Additionally, you can use the arrow keys to go in their respective directions in increments.

To zoom in or out with your mouse, you can scroll up to zoom in or scroll down to zoom out; you will zoom wherever your mouse pointer is with this method. You may also press the = and - keys to zoom in and out respectively. Finally, you may click the plus and minus symbols on the top left of the viewer to zoom in and out respectively.

Once you find your service of choice, you can click on its marker to learn more about it.

What info does PCSV show me?

PCSV shows you the location of a service, what kind of service it is, and the official link to the service’s website. More info may be added to PCSV’s entries as needed.
